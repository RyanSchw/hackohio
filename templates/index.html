FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is BVE?

BVE is a project developed for Hack OHI/O 2019.

The project aims to take nothing seriously while teaching something new to the participants. This idea was chosen as a good reason to learn to use the Azure platform.

How accurate is it?

According to our training data, not very. The model itself reports under 30% accuracy, but this is likely due to the random and spontaneous nature of vines. We've done our best to hone in on what makes a vine good, but at the end of the day, 25 hours isn't enough to get all the data we require.

How do I use the features of BVE?

It's quite simple! Just hit the upload button at the top of the page and insert your video. Our product will do the rest.

How much does the service cost?

Nothing! Charging people for this app would be a crime. We simply enjoy the development journey and want to show off what we do.

I have technical problem, what do I do?

Read the source code.

This project will not be supported in the future, so it is up to you to fix anything you desire. You can find the project on the GitHub.
